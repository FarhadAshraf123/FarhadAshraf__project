Crysta: The Sultan of Style.

The Toyota Innova Crysta 2.8L Diesel was widely considered the most powerful and effortless version of the Crysta lineup before being discontinued in 2020. It was primarily paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission, making it a favorite for long-distance cruising and city comfort.

The 2.8L Diesel engine produced around 174 horsepower and 360 Nm of torque, providing ample power for both city driving and highway cruising. It was known for its smooth performance, making it a popular choice among families and those who valued comfort and reliability.

Features

- Powerful 2.8L Diesel Engine
- Smooth 6-Speed Automatic Transmission
- Spacious and Comfortable Interior
- Advanced Safety Features
- Stylish Exterior Design
